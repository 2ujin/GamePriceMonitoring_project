"Make indie games inside an indie game"

Summary

This is a story about a young and naive indie developer.

The protagonist is a person who have a dream of publishing his own game. Just like many other newbee developers, he tries to master those skills needed for developing. In addition, he will need to figure out how the world of gamers works.
Is him simple and naive enough to have an indie dream?
