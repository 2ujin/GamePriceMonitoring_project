About This Game

Angel Light The Elven Truce
3rd person Fantasy Action Adventure! Become Angelore! an Elf princess willing to make peace with the Dark Elves! Fight for Peace Now!
Also you will play as some Dark Elves main characters and The Knight Templars!

Thanks to Bat Prince for making this awesome video!

The game is focused in combat and the story mode. The game is meant to be hard, there is no respawn or save point, but we added a checkpoint to be closer of some of the other half of the game objectives.

The Player will have to complete Quest that increase the "INFLUENCE" over a map, completing these quest are part of the story and it will unlock areas of the map and Steam Achievements!
